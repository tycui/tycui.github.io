## About Me
 I'm a first-year Doctoral Candidate at [Probabilistic Machine Learning group](https://research.cs.aalto.fi/pml/), [Aalto University](http://www.aalto.fi/en/), working with Samuel Kaski and  Pekka Marttinen. I'm interested in probabilistic inference, machine learning, bioinformatics, quantitative finance, and many more things related to _Data Science_.

More about me [here](./more_about_me.html).
